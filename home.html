Important FAQ

Can I donate goods instead of money?

While goods are appreciated, financial donations allow relief organizations to be more flexible and efficient in purchasing the most needed items locally. If you'd still like to donate goods, check with organizations to see what they are currently accepting.

Why should I donate to support the Noakhali flood victims?

Noakhali has been severely affected by destructive floods, leaving thousands homeless and without basic necessities. By donating, you provide immediate relief in the form of food, clean water, shelter, and medical care, helping families survive and recover from the disaster.

Why should I donate to help flood victims in Feni?

Your donation can provide lifesaving assistance to those affected by the flood. Immediate support is needed for food, shelter, water, and medical care. Every contribution, no matter the size, can make a significant difference in the lives of the people suffering from this disaster.

What is the Quota Movement of 2024?

The Quota Movement of 2024 is a mass protest by students and activists against certain quota policies in public sector jobs and higher education in [specific country]. The movement has led to clashes with law enforcement, resulting in injuries to many protestors.

How can I spread the word about the need for donations?

You can help by sharing information on social media, organizing local fundraising events, and talking to your friends and family about the situation. The more people are aware, the more help the flood victims can receive.
